The Food Lover History

more than +25 Yaer of experience

Food Lover is the favorite application and website for those who love to eat in Palestine, and also the easiest, simplest, and most economical way to order your food from more than 200 restaurants in all of Palestine. It includes Nablus restaurants, Ramallah restaurants, Bethlehem restaurants, Jenin restaurants, Tulkarm restaurants and other cities.

One of the most important reasons that force you to order from Food Lover is the comfort of phone calls with restaurants and to browse the menu of the restaurant you love at any time and anywhere, every day there are offers and discounts on food.
